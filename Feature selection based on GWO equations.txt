W(iter+1) = P(iter) − A * Di

Di = | C * P(t) − W(t) |

A = 2 b * r1 − b
C = 2 r2
r1, r2 random vectors between [0,1]

W(iter+1) = (W1+W2+W3) / 3 
W1 = | Wα − A1 * Diα |
W2 = | Wβ − A2 * Diβ |
W3 = | Wδ − A3 * Diδ |

Diα = |C1 * Wα − W|
Diβ = |C2 * Wβ − W|
Diβ = |C3 * Wβ − W|

b = 2 − iter*(2 / MAXIter)
